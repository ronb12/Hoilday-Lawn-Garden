Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2024

Holliday's Lawn & Garden is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing address
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing history, and payment records
  • Service Information: Service history, preferences, and property details
  • Usage Data: Website interaction data, cookies, and analytics
  • Communication Records: Emails, messages, and support interactions

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our lawn care and garden services
  • Process payments and send receipts
  • Communicate about your account and services
  • Improve our website and service offerings
  • Send service updates and marketing communications (with consent)
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist in our operations
  • Payment processors for secure transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business partners with your consent

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Request data deletion
  • Manage cookie preferences
  • View and download your data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure payment processing
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
