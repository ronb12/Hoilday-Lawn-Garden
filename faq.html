Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

📍

What areas do you serve?

We provide lawn care and landscaping services throughout New Orleans and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your neighborhood.

📅

How often should I schedule lawn maintenance?

We recommend weekly or bi-weekly maintenance for optimal lawn health. However, we can customize a schedule based on your needs.

🚨

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we provide emergency services for storm damage and urgent maintenance needs. Contact us immediately for assistance.

💳

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and online payments through our secure payment system.

📋

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free estimates for all our services. Contact us to schedule a consultation.

⏰

What is your cancellation policy?

We require 24-hour notice for service cancellations. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule.

📜

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, we are fully licensed and insured for all our services. We can provide proof of insurance upon request.

🌤️

Do you offer seasonal services?

Yes, we provide seasonal services including spring cleanup, fall maintenance, and winter preparation.
