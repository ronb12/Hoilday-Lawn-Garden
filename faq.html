Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our lawn care and landscaping services. Get expert advice on maintenance, scheduling, and more.

Services & Offerings

What lawn care services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive lawn care services including mowing, edging, trimming, fertilization, weed control, pest management, aeration, overseeding, and seasonal cleanups. We also provide landscaping design and installation services.

Do you offer one-time services or only recurring maintenance?

We offer both one-time services and recurring maintenance plans. Whether you need a single cleanup, ongoing weekly maintenance, or seasonal services, we can accommodate your needs with flexible scheduling options.

What areas do you service?

We service the greater New Orleans area and surrounding communities. Contact us with your specific location to confirm we service your area and to get a free estimate.

Do you handle both residential and commercial properties?

Yes, we provide lawn care services for both residential and commercial properties. Our team is experienced in maintaining various property types and sizes, from small residential yards to large commercial landscapes.

Scheduling & Appointments

How do I schedule a service?

You can schedule a service by calling us at (504) 717-1887, emailing us at 7holliday@gmail.com, or using our online booking system. We'll work with you to find a convenient time that fits your schedule.

What are your service hours?

We typically work Monday through Friday, 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We can accommodate special scheduling needs for commercial clients or emergency situations.

How far in advance should I schedule?

For regular maintenance, we recommend scheduling at least a week in advance. For one-time services or seasonal work, 2-3 weeks notice is ideal, especially during peak seasons (spring and fall).

What happens if it rains on my scheduled service day?

We monitor weather conditions and will reschedule your service if heavy rain or severe weather would affect the quality of our work. We'll contact you to reschedule for the next available time slot.

Pricing & Payment

How much do your services cost?

Pricing varies based on property size, service type, and frequency. We provide free estimates after assessing your property. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer any discounts or package deals?

Yes, we offer discounts for recurring maintenance plans, seasonal packages, and referrals. We also provide special pricing for commercial clients and larger properties. Ask us about our current promotions!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, checks, credit cards, and online payments. We can set up automatic billing for recurring services, and we offer convenient online payment options through our customer portal.

When is payment due?

Payment is typically due upon completion of service. For recurring maintenance plans, we offer monthly billing options. Commercial clients may be eligible for net 30 payment terms.

Lawn Care & Maintenance

How often should I have my lawn mowed?

During the growing season (spring and summer), most lawns benefit from weekly mowing. In fall and winter, mowing frequency can be reduced to every 2-3 weeks. We'll adjust based on your grass type and growth rate.

What's the best time to fertilize my lawn?

For warm-season grasses common in Louisiana, the best times to fertilize are late spring (May) and late summer (August/September). We'll create a customized fertilization schedule based on your specific grass type and soil conditions.

How do you handle weeds and pests?

We use integrated pest management techniques, including pre-emergent weed control, targeted herbicide applications, and natural pest control methods when possible. We'll identify specific issues and recommend the most effective treatment.

Do you provide seasonal lawn care advice?

Yes! We provide seasonal care tips and recommendations for your specific lawn type. We'll help you understand what your lawn needs during each season and can adjust our services accordingly.
