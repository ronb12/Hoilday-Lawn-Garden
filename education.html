Lawn Care Education & Tips

Expert advice and seasonal tips to keep your lawn and garden healthy, beautiful, and thriving all year round.

Seasonal Lawn Care Tips

  • Spring: Aerate your lawn, apply pre-emergent weed control, and fertilize for new growth.
  • Summer: Mow regularly, water deeply but infrequently, and watch for pests and diseases.
  • Fall: Overseed bare spots, apply fall fertilizer, and keep leaves off the grass.
  • Winter: Minimize foot traffic, keep the lawn clear of debris, and prepare equipment for spring.

Best Practices

  • Mowing: Never cut more than 1/3 of the grass blade at a time. Keep mower blades sharp.
  • Watering: Water early in the morning. Lawns typically need 1" of water per week.
  • Fertilizing: Use a slow-release fertilizer and follow recommended application rates.
  • Weed Control: Pull weeds early and use mulch to suppress new growth.
